## Rotation matricies
`rotx(ϕ)`:

$$
\mathbf{R}_x(\phi) =
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & \cos \phi & -\sin \phi \\
0 & \sin \phi & \cos \phi
\end{pmatrix}
$$



`roty(θ)`:

$$
\mathbf{R}_y(\theta) =
\begin{pmatrix}
\cos \theta & 0 & \sin \theta \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
-\sin \theta & 0 & \cos \theta
\end{pmatrix}
$$

`rotz(ψ)`:

$$
\mathbf{R}_z(\psi) =
\begin{pmatrix}
\cos \psi & -\sin \psi & 0 \\
\sin \psi & \cos \psi & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix}
$$
